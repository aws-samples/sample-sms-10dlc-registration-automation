Brand Vetting (Optional but Recommended)

Vetting can increase your throughput limits. Without vetting: 75 msg/min to AT&T, 2,000 msg/day to T-Mobile.

$40 non-refundable fee. Vetting scores are NOT applied retroactively to existing campaigns — vet your brand BEFORE creating campaigns. The vetting registration has no field definitions; it's just create, associate the brand, and submit.
