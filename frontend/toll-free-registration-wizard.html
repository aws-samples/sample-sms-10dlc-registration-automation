🧹 Cleanup — Removing Resources After Testing

To avoid ongoing charges, delete the resources created by this wizard:

  1. Delete the phone number: aws pinpoint-sms-voice-v2 release-phone-number --phone-number-id <PHONE_NUMBER_ID>
  2. Delete the toll-free registration: aws pinpoint-sms-voice-v2 delete-registration --registration-id <REGISTRATION_ID>

Note: Registration fees are non-refundable. Monthly phone number lease charges stop after the number is released.
